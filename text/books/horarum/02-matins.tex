{Susċitátus e sòmno et lècto a te, Dòmine, illúmina mèntem meam et àperi cor meum et làbia mea ut càntem tìbi, Sancta Trínitas, «~Sanctus, sanctus, sanctus es tu, Deus.~» Per oraçiónes Deíparae mizerére nostri.}

\prayerVersicle{Orémus.}\vspaceLarge

% O Lord God Almighty, who has enabled us to reach the beginning of this day: save us today by your power, so that on this day we may turn aside to no sin, but that our words may always proceed, our thoughts and works be directed, to the doing of your justice; through Jesus Christ our Lord.
\prayerText{\firstLetter{D}òmine Deus omnípotens, qui ad prinċìpium hujus diéi nos perveníre feċìsti: hódie virtúte tua sàlva nos, ut in hac die ad nullum peccátum declinémus, sed semper ad tuam justìçiam faċièndam nostra proċédant elóquia, dirigàntur coġitaçiónes et ópera; per Dòminum nostrum Jesum Christum.}

\prayerResponse{Amen.}\vspaceLarge

% Deign to direct and sanctify, rule and govern, O Lord God, King of heaven and earth, today our hearts and bodies, our senses, words, and deeds in your law, and in the works of your commandments: so that here and eternally, with your help, we may be worthy to be safe and free, O Savior of the world, who lives and reigns unto ages of ages.
\prayerText{\firstLetter{D}irìgere et sanctificáre, règere et guvernáre dignáre, Dòmine Deus, Rex ċaeli et tèrrae, hódie corda et córpora nostra, sènsüs, sermónes et àctüs nostros in lege tua, et in opèribus mandatórum tuórum, ut hic et in aetèrnum, te auxiliànte, sàlvi et líberi esse mereámur, Salvátor mùndi, qui vivis et regnas in sáecula saeculórum.}

\prayerResponse{Amen.}\vspaceLarge

% Morning prayer of St. Basil
% We bless you, O God, Most High and Lord of Mercy, who always work great and inscrutable things, glorious and innumerable wonders with us. You grant sleep to us for rest from our infirmities, and quiet from the burdens of the flesh that is laboring greatly. We give thanks to you, because you have not lost us on account of our sins, but have remained in your love; and when we had fallen into despair, you raised us up to glorify your power. Therefore, we beseech your incomparable goodness: illuminate the eyes of our understanding, and lift our minds from the heavy sleep of laziness. Open our mouth and fill it with your praise, so that without distraction we may be able to sing and confess that you are God, glorified in all and through all, the Eternal Father, with your Only-Begotten Son, and your Most Holy, good, and life-giving Spirit, now and always, and forever and ever.
\prayerText{\firstLetter{B}enedícimus te, Deus, Altìssime et Dòmine mizericórdiae, qui sèmper nobíscum operáris màgna et inscrutabìlia, gloriósa ac mirabìlia innumerabìlia. Tu nobïs sòmnum larġíris ad rèquiem ab infirmitátibus nostrïs, et quiétem ab onèribus carnis multum laboràntis. Gráçias tìbi àgimus, quia non perdidìsti nos pròpter peccáta nostra, sed in tua dilecçióne permansìsti; et quum in desperaçiónem lapsi essémus, erexìsti nos ad glorificàndam virtútem tuam. Ìdeo tuam bonitátem incomparábilem deprecámur: illúmina òculos intelléctüs nostri, et lèva mèntes nostras de gràvi sòmno desìdiae. Àperi os nostrum et ìmple ìllud laude tua, ut sine distracçióne valeámus cantáre et confitéri te esse Deum, glorificátum in òmnibus et per òmnia, Pàtrem aetèrnum, cum Uniġènito Fílio tuo, et Spíritu tuo sanctìssimo, bono et vivìfico, nunc et sèmper, et in sáecula saeculórum.}

\prayerResponse{Amen.}\vspaceLarge
