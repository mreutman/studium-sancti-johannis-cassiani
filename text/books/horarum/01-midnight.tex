\sectionTitle{Ad Medianocturnum}\vspaceMedium

\prayerVersicleTriple
% Having been awakened from sleep, let us fall down before you, O Benevolent One (Benign One), and let us sing to you, O Most Powerful One, the angelic hymn, "Holy, holy, holy are you, O God." Through the prayers of the Theotokos, have mercy on us.
{Experǧefàcti e sòmno coram te pročidámus, Benìgne, et tìbi čantámus, Potentìssime, hỳmnum anǧèlicum, «~Sanctus, sanctus, sanctus es tu, Deus.~» Per oraçiónes Deíparae mizerére nostri.}
% Having been raised (awakened) from sleep and bed by you, O Lord, illuminate my mind and open my heart and my lips so that I may sing to you, O Holy Trinity, "Holy, holy, holy are you, O God." Through the prayers of the Theotokos, have mercy on us.
{Susčitátus e sòmno et lècto a te, Dòmine, illúmina mèntem meam et àperi cor meum et làbia mea ut càntem tìbi, Sancta Trínitas, «~Sanctus, sanctus, sanctus es tu, Deus.~» Per oraçiónes Deíparae mizerére nostri.}
% Suddenly the Judge will come and the works of all will be revealed. Truly, with fear we cry out in the middle of the night, "Holy, holy, holy are you, O God." Through the prayers of the Theotokos, have mercy on us.
{Sùbito Judex vèniet et revelabùntur ópera cunctórum. Cum timóre vèro clamámus mèdia nòcte, «~Sanctus, sanctus, sanctus es tu, Deus.~» Per oraçiónes Deíparae mizerére nostri.}\vspaceSmall

% Glory to you, O King, God Almighty, who through your divine and gracious providence granted that I, an unworthy sinner, might rise from sleep and enter into your holy house. Receive, O Lord, the voice of my prayer just as you receive those [prayers] of your Saints and Angelic Powers; and grant that praise may be offered to you not through my defiled lips, but from a pure heart and a humble spirit, so that I also, with the lamp of my soul bright, may be made a companion of the prudent virgins, and may glorify you, God the Word, Who are glorified in the Father and the Spirit."
\prayerText{\firstLetter{G}lória tìbi, Rex, Deus omnìpotens, qui per tuam divínam et píam providènçiam cončessìsti ut ego, indìgnus peccátor, a sòmno sùrgerem et introírem in domum tuam sanctam. Àcčipe, Dòmine, vočem oraçiónis meae sicut àcčipis ìllas Sanctórum tuórum et potestátum Anǧelicárum~; et praesta ut non per làbia mea inquináta, sed ex corde puro et spíritu húmili, laus tìbi offerátur, ut et ego, cum lučèrna ànimae meae clara, sóčius effìčiar vírǧinum prudèntium, et glorìficem te, Deum Vèrbum, qui in Pàtre et Spíritu glorificáris.}\vspaceSmall

\prayerResponse{Amen.}\vspaceMedium
