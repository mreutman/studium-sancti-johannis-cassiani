\prayerVersicle{Orémus.}\vspaceMedium

% Now, O Lord our God, grant unto us in the sleep that follows, rest of body and soul, and keep us from the dark sleep of sin and from every dark and nocturnal sensuality. Restrain the impulses of the passions, extinguish the fiery darts of the evil one, craftily hurled against us; soothe the disturbances of our flesh, and lull to sleep all earthly and material thoughts. Grant unto us, O most merciful God, a watchful mind, a chaste reason, a sober heart, and a tranquil sleep, free from every evil fantasy. At the hour of prayer, awaken us, that being confirmed in Thy commandments, we may be ever mindful of Thy judgments. And grant, we beseech Thee, that through the whole night we may be able to glorify Thee, so that we may sing to, bless, and glorify Thy most honored and majestic name: of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit, now and ever, and unto ages of ages.
\prayerText{\firstLetter{N}unc, Dòmine Deus noster, cončéde nobīs in sòmno sequènti, requiem còrporis et ànimae, et custódi nos a tenebróso sòmno peccáti et ab omni obscura ac nocturna sensuali. Cóhibe ìmpetūs passiónum, exstìngüe tela malìgni igníta, dolóse còntra nos emìssa; mìtiga turbaçiónem carnis nostrae, et sopi coǧitaçiónes terrénas ac materiáles. Práesta nobīs, Deus piìssime, mèntem viǧilàntem, raçiónem càstam, cor sóbrium, et sòmnum tranquìllum, ab òmni fantàzia màla líberum. Ad horam oraçiónis èxčita nos, ut in mandátīs tuīs confirmáti judičiórum tuórum simus sèmper mèmores. Et práesta, quaesumus, ut per totam nòctem te glorificáre valeámus, ita ut nomen tuum honoratìssimum ac majestáticum cantémus, benedicámus, et glorificémus~: Pàtris, et Fílii, et Spíritūs Sancti, nunc et sèmper et in sáecula saeculórum.}\vspaceMedium

\prayerResponse{Amen.}\vspaceMedium

% Lord, Lover of mankind, forgive those who hate and persecute us; do good to those who do good to us, and grant them all things necessary for salvation and eternal life. Visit the sick, and grant them health. Guide those at sea; travel with those who journey. Assist our authorities in every good work for the peace of the world. Grant remission of sins to those who serve us and those who have mercy on us. In the multitude of Thy mercy, have mercy on those who, through us unworthy ones, entreat Thee for themselves. Remember, O Lord, our departed brothers and sisters, and grant them rest where the light of Thy countenance shines upon them. Remember, O Lord, our brothers who are in captivity, and preserve them from every evil. Remember those who do good to Thy churches, and grant them salvation and eternal life. Remember also, O Lord, us humble and sinful and unworthy servants of Thine. Enlighten our minds with the light of Thy knowledge and lead us in the way of Thy commandments; through the intercessions of our most pure Lady, Mother of God and ever-virgin Mary, and of all Thy saints, for blessed art Thou unto the ages of ages.
\prayerText{\firstLetter{D}òmine, Amátor hòminum, remìtte odièntibus et persequèntibus nos; benéfače eīs qui benefáčiunt nobīs, et dóna eīs òmnia ad salútem et vitam aetèrnam nečessária. Vísita infìrmos, et praesta sanitátem ìllīs. Díriǧe navigàntes; comitáre ìter fačièntes. Assìste potestátibus nostrīs in òmni ópere bono ad pačem mùndi. Praesta remissiónem peccatórum eīs qui sèrviunt nobīs et qui nostri mizerèntur. In multitúdine mizeraçiónis tuae, mizerére eórum qui per nos indìgnos te deprecàntur pro se. Memènto, Dòmine, fratrum et sorórum nostrárum defunctórum, et rèquiem eīs cončéde, ubi super eos fùlǧet lux vùltus tui. Memènto, Dómine, fratrum nostrórum qui sunt in captivitáte, et consèrva eos ab òmni màlo. Memènto eórum qui benefàčiunt ecclésiīs tuīs, et dóna ìllīs salútem et vitam aetèrnam. Memènto ètiam, Dòmine, nostrum humìlium et peccatórum et indignórum servórum tuórum. Illúmina mèntes nostras lúmine sciènçiae tuae et dedúce nos in via mandatórum tuórum; per interčessiónes Dòminae nostrae purìssimae, Dei Matris ac semper Vìrǧinis Maríae, et òmnium sanctórum tuórum, quia benedìctus es in sáecula saeculórum.}\vspaceMedium
