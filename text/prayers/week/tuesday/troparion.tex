% The memory of the just is celebrated with hymns of praise, but for you, the testimony of the Lord suffices, O Precursor. Truly you have been shown to be the most honored of the Prophets, because you were found worthy to baptize in the Jordan the One who was foretold. Then, having endured the struggle for the truth even unto death, you rejoiced to bring the good tidings even to those who were in hell: that God has appeared in the flesh, He who takes away the sin of the world and grants us great mercy.
