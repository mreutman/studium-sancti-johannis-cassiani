% Apostles, Prophets, and all Martyrs, holy Hierarchs, the Righteous and the Blessed: who have fought the good fight, the true faith having been kept, you have boldness before our Savior. Intercede for us with Him who reigns in eternity, we pray now, that He may save our souls.
Apòstoli, Profétae, et òmnes Màrtyres, * sancti Hierárchae, Justi et Beáti * quī bonum čertámen čertavìstis, fidem veram servànta, * ante Salvatórem nostrum fidúčiam habétis. * Interčédite pro nobīs ei qui in aetèrno regnat, * orámus nunc, ut ànimas nostras sàlvet.
