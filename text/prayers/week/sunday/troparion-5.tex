% Let us, the faithful, praise and worship the Word, co-eternal with the Father and the Spirit, born for our salvation from the Virgin; for He willed to be lifted up on the Cross in the flesh, to endure death, and to raise the dead by His glorious Resurrection.
Čelebrémus atque adorémus nos fidéles verbum, coaetèrnum Pàtri una cum Spíritu Sancto, natum de Vìrǧine ad salútem nostram; quia vòluit in càrne cručem asčèndere, et sustinére mortem, et mórtuos susčitáre gloriósa sua resurrecçióne.
