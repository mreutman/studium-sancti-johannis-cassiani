% When you descended unto death, O Life Immortal, with the lightning of thy divinity thou slayed death. And when out from the depths thou didst raise the dead, all the powers of heaven cried out: O Giver of Life, Christ our God, glory to thee!
Quum ad mortem desčendìsti, Vita Immortális, fùlgore divinitátis tuae infernum peremìsti. Quum ètiam susčitavìsti de profùndīs mórtuos, òmnes čaelórum Virtútes clamábant: «~Dator Vitae, Christe Deus noster, glória tìbi.~»
